# Omar Pizza — Neapolitan Calculator

**Live app:** https://xk5fvhmcht-oss.github.io/omar-pizza/

A science-verified Neapolitan pizza dough calculator. Built as a Progressive Web App — install it on your iPhone home screen and it works offline.

## Features

- **Four fermentation modes** — Rapid, Slow, Sourdough, and Biga
- **Biga math** — yeast calculated dynamically based on actual room temp + cold fermentation hours, not lookup tables. Calibrated against the Giorilli standard.
- **Desired Dough Temperature** — water temperature calculated for both biga (Giorilli formula) and final dough (Hamelman 3-factor/4-factor)
- **Diastatic malt toggle** — 0.5% of flour, with fermentation activity adjustment (×1.18)
- **Newtonian cooling model** — calculates temper time to pull balls from fridge
- **Bake journal** — save notes from each bake, stored locally on your device
- **Two themes** — Artisan and Professional
- **PWA** — works offline, installable on iOS via Safari share sheet

## Ingredients

Pure Neapolitan. Four ingredients only:

- Tipo 00 flour (or bread flour)
- Water
- Sea salt — 2.8% (AVPN range: 2.5–3%)
- Yeast — fresh, active dry, or instant

## Dough Balls

- Range: 220–300g in 5g steps
- Quick pills: 230g (thin) / 250g (classic, AVPN standard) / 270g (generous)
- Default: 6 balls at 250g

## Ovens Supported

- Home steel at 550°F
- Gozney Dome at 700–900°F

## Mixers Supported

- Hand mix (default for sourdough)
- KitchenAid
- Ooni Halo Pro spiral (default)

## Install on iPhone

1. Open in Safari
2. Tap the Share button
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Works offline after first load
