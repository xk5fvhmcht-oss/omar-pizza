Mix biga flour + biga water + yeast until just combined — shaggy and rough is correct, do not develop gluten. Dissolve yeast in the biga water first. Mix by hand or spiral mixer at slow speed for 3–4 min. Target biga water temperature: — · Place in a lightly oiled tall container. Cover with perforated cling film — the biga needs to breathe. 🍋 Before you mix: pull your starter from the fridge, feed it, and wait for peak activity — it should dome and show bubbles throughout. This is the most important step. The dough follows the starter, not the clock.
🤲 Mix: autolyse flour + most of the water 30–60 min. Add starter, mix well, then add salt dissolved in reserved water. Develop with stretch & fold sets every 30 min for the first 2 hours — 3 to 4 sets. The dough tells you when it's ready: it becomes smooth, holds its shape, and passes the windowpane test.
🔴 Ball & cold proof: divide, shape into tight balls, place in oiled containers, seal, and refrigerate. Pull when the dough has relaxed and yields softly to a finger press. Temper at room temperature before shaping.
